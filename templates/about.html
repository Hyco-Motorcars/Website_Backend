Who Is HYCO?

Hyco Motors is a family owned and operated business located in Monroe Wisconsin. We own more than 120 collectible cars ranging from the 1924 Chrysler B-70 Touring to the 2008 Maserati Grand Turismo. Select vehicles are offered for sale while others are for driving and event rentals.

After retiring from the stressful career as a private equity investor, owner Jeff Cappel decided to invest in his passion of collectible vehicles.
